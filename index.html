Harmonia

Stronger teams through shared responsibility

Non-heirarchical team structures produce teams with more motivation and higher morale. But without a traditional manager, how can you ensure that tasks are really getting done?

Harmonia is our answer. It captures the tasks and chores that a team needs to handle, and intelligently assigns responsibilities for those tasks as required, promoting engagement across the team whilst giving visibility of progress across the whole team.
